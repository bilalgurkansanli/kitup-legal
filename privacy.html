Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how KitUp ("the App"), a private content automation tool operated by its owner ("we", "us", or "the operator"), handles information.

1. Scope

KitUp is a personal automation tool used only by the operator. It does not provide public user accounts and does not knowingly collect personal data from visitors or third parties.

2. Information We Process

The App may process the following data solely for automation purposes:

  • TikTok OAuth tokens and account identifiers authorized by the operator
  • Generated video files, captions, and metadata before publishing
  • Technical logs required to operate the pipeline (errors, timestamps, job status)

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No public user registration data
  • No payment or billing data through this App
  • No analytics or advertising tracking of website visitors
  • No sale or sharing of personal data with third parties for marketing

4. Third-Party Services

KitUp connects to third-party APIs (including TikTok) only to publish content on accounts authorized by the operator. Data shared with those platforms is governed by their privacy policies, including the TikTok Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

Credentials and tokens are stored securely in private server configuration files accessible only to the operator. Reasonable technical measures are used to protect this data, but no system is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

OAuth tokens and pipeline logs are retained only as long as needed to operate the App. The operator may revoke platform access at any time through the respective platform settings.

7. Your Rights

Because KitUp does not offer public user accounts, data subject requests from third parties are generally not applicable. The operator may contact us directly regarding any privacy questions.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the latest revision.
