Official website for the KitUp application

KitUp creates slow, immersive English book summaries and publishes them to YouTube, podcast feeds, Instagram Reels, and TikTok. This site describes the KitUp service, legal policies, and developer integration details.

About KitUp

KitUp is operated by Bilal Şanlı as a personal media automation project. The application generates narration, visuals, and short-form clips from curated book scripts, then publishes finished videos to connected social accounts through official platform APIs.

KitUp is not a public consumer signup product. There is no user registration on this website. Content is published only to channels owned and authorized by the operator.

Services

  • Long-form YouTube book summary videos (English)
  • YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and TikTok clips
  • English podcast episodes via public RSS
  • Automated publishing through YouTube, Meta, and TikTok developer APIs

Channels & content

  • YouTube — KitUp book summary channel
  • Podcast (RSS) — English audio episodes
