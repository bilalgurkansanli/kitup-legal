KitUp

KitUp is a personal media project that creates slow, immersive book summaries in English and publishes them to video and audio channels.

What we publish

  • Long-form book summary videos on YouTube
  • Short-form clips for YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and TikTok
  • English podcast episodes (RSS)

Channels

  • YouTube — book summary videos (operator channel)
  • Podcast (RSS) — English audio episodes

Automation & APIs

Content is produced with a private automation pipeline on the operator's own infrastructure. KitUp uses official platform APIs (including TikTok for Developers) only to publish media to accounts authorized by the operator. This website documents the project for app review and legal transparency; it does not offer public sign-up or login.
